Background Remover

This Background Remover tool allows users to easily upload an image, process it, and remove its background automatically. Simply select an image from your device, and the tool uses the Remove.bg API to detect and remove the background. Once the background is removed, both the original and processed images are displayed side by side for comparison. The process is quick and seamless, offering a simple way to extract the subject from an image without complex editing software. Ideal for creating transparent background images for various uses.
